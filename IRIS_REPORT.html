Executive Summary

Iris is a contemplative flower-trimming game built in Unity 6 (URP) that blends cozy apartment life simulation, dating mechanics, and meditative craft gameplay. Developed as a thesis project over 5.5 months — from flower-trimming physics prototypes in September 2025 through an intensive apartment systems sprint — it has reached remarkable technical maturity.

The target experience is 2–6 hours of intimate, emotionally resonant gameplay — comparable to Coffee Talk, Venba, and Wanderstop. Players read the morning newspaper, prepare their apartment, host a date, then trim flowers in quiet reflection.
